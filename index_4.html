Meet us

Hi, we're Julia & Alex

We're a Maryland-based photography duo passionate about capturing life's most meaningful moments. From senior portraits and couples sessions to group photos and beautiful landscapes, we love creating photos that feel genuine, warm, and timeless.

When we're not behind the camera, you can usually find us traveling, hiking, spending time with animals, or enjoying quality time together. Photography allows us to connect with people, tell stories, and preserve memories that last forever — and we're so excited to share that passion with you!

— Julia & Alex
